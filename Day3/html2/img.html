Dr B.R.Ambedkar

The statue is 125 feet tall and stands on a 50 feet high plinth, thus its total height is 175 feet.[2][3] After the Statue of Social Justice in Vijayawada, this is the tallest statue of B. R. Ambedkar in the world. It is the fifth tallest, and third tallest standing statue in India.[4] CM K. Chandrashekar Rao, Prakash Ambedkar infront of 125 feet Ambedkar Statue in Hyderabad on 14 April 2023 The then Chief Minister of Telangana K. Chandrasekhar Rao laid the foundation stone of Ambedkar's 125 feet tall statue on 14 April 2016 on the occasion of his 125th birth anniversary. The foundation stone was laid in 2016, but the construction of the statue began in 2021.[5] After 7 years, the CM unveiled this giant statue of Ambedkar on 14 April 2023 on the occasion of his 132nd birth anniversary.[6] The statue was designed by Ram V. Sutar.[7]
